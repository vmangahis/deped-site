Laguna Cluster Education Leaders conducts consultative meeting.

By  Rafaello Luis L. Galman   / March 21, 2021, 11:28 p.m.

Regional Director Wilfredo Cabral, together with Assistant Regional Director Ruth Fuentes conducted a consultative meetings with Laguna Cluster Education Leaders at SDO Biñan. All Schools Division Superintendents and Assistant Schools Division Superintendents of Laguna cluster together with the Administrative Officer V of Cabuyao participated in the said meeting. They tackled challenges and discussed solutions for topics such as the proposal to extend the current Academic Calendar Year, Concerns on the Delivery of Self-Learning Modules, Availability of Tablets for learners, Position of the SDOs on the Procurement of Quarter 4 SLMs, Concerns on unfilled positions, and Using of Schools as Vaccination Centers.
