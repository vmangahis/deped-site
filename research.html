Division Research Agenda


SDO Biñan City strives to foster and uphold synergy, innovation and collaboration among it’s personnel. Develop meaningful researches that are responsive to the emerging needs of clients.

The division is committed on it’s aim of crafting researches for policy development, decision-making, and basis of planning and development of programs, projects, and activities relative to Focus on Learning, Reaching the Marginalized, Education Financing, Safe Operations, and Well-Being and Protection of students, parents, teaching and non-teaching personnel and stakeholders.

SDO Biñan City is firm in it’s belief that research, as a product of creative and innovative minds of it’s personnel is a never-ending cycle of innovation and improvement to respond to the needs of the community.
